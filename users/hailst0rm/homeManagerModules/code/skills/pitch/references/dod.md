# Definition of done — pitch

A stranger should be able to open `recap.html`, understand the business in five minutes, open the landing page locally, see the launch asset, and walk away either convinced or precisely informed about why they are not.

Grade the run against every item. Fix anything failing before stopping. Nothing may be a placeholder pretending to be finished work. A cut must be logged in `build-log.md` with a reason.

- [ ] Every guardrail held — nothing published, no new spend, nothing fabricated.
- [ ] Every factual claim in the package (brand, landing, recap) traces to a live URL; inferences are labelled.
- [ ] The landing page renders, has a working fake-door CTA, and is screenshot-verified on **mobile and desktop**.
- [ ] The launch asset renders and you actually opened/watched it; the media path is logged.
- [ ] `DESIGN.md` is complete enough that a stranger could make a new on-brand asset from it alone.
- [ ] The red-team ran and its surviving objections are visible in `recap.html`.
- [ ] 1–2 optional deliverables shipped, each polished, chosen for fit.
- [ ] `recap.html` opens, explains the business in five minutes, and every link resolves.
- [ ] `build-log.md` records every self-answered question and every cut with its reason.
- [ ] The output path was printed for the user.
